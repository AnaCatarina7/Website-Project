Live Reading Sessions

Come join our live reading sessions every week!

Information:

We are a group of book enthusiasts who gather monthly to discuss a wide range of literature. From classic novels to contemporary works, from mystery thrillers to historical non-fiction. In our group, we cherish the diversity of thoughts and perspectives that each member brings to the table. Our discussions are not merely about dissecting the plot or critiquing the characters, but they extend to understanding the underlying themes, the cultural contexts, and the philosophical questions that the books provoke. Each month, we select a book through a democratic voting process, ensuring that every voice is heard and every genre gets its fair share of attention. The chosen book then becomes our companion for the next few weeks, as we delve into its pages, immersing ourselves in the world crafted by the author. Our meetings are lively, filled with passionate debates, insightful observations, and sometimes, even heated disagreements. But at the end of the day, we all share a common love for literature and a mutual respect for each other’s opinions. These gatherings are not just about books, but also about building a community of like-minded individuals who appreciate the power of words and the magic of storytelling. We also invite authors, scholars, and industry professionals for guest talks, providing us with a deeper understanding of the literary world. These sessions offer a unique opportunity to engage with the minds behind the books we love and gain insights into the creative process.

Week 1:

Book Selection and Announcement

Members suggest books for the upcoming month. A vote is held, and the book with the most votes is selected. The selected book is announced to the members.

Week 2-3:

Reading Time

Members read the selected book at their own pace. Optional: Mid-month check-in to share initial thoughts and reactions.

Week 4:

Discussion Preparation

Members prepare points they would like to discuss, questions they have, or passages they found interesting.

End of Month:

Book Discussion Meeting

A virtual meeting is held to discuss the book. This could be broken down as follows:

  • First 15 minutes: Casual conversation, welcoming members.
  • Next 45 minutes: Open discussion about the book.
  • Next 30 minutes: Discussion on specific themes, characters, or events in the book.
  • Last 15 minutes: Wrap-up, sharing final thoughts, and announcing the next book (if already decided).

Guest Talks (Quarterly/Bi-annually):

Schedule guest talks with authors, scholars, or industry professionals. These can be separate events or part of the regular book discussion meetings, depending on the availability of the guest.
